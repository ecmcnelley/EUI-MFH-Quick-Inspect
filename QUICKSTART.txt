## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### Camera Not Working
- Check browser permissions
- Use Safari on iOS

- Use Safari on iOS (not Chrome)

- Check internet on first visit
- Clear browser cache
- Wait 2 min after GitHub Pages enable
- Hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R)

- Check storage space
- Reduce photo resolution
- Take fewer photos
- Try different browser
